Influence of different planets on all of us.


The eight planets that make up our solar system are really lesser-known locations. Within your birth chart, the planets Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto all have various meanings.

Moon

Your moon sign corresponds to the month and day of your birth, yet it might be difficult to match your moon sign precisely because it changes frequently. Your emotions and the side of yourself that you keep concealed are represented by moon signs.

Mercury

Mercury is renowned as the planet that "thinks." You may be aware of the Mercury retrograde phenomenon, which has become a running gag in the astrological world. For many people, this experience is extremely challenging since it disrupts our ability to interact as humans.

Venus

Venus is the planet of "love." It usually refers to physical characteristics, hobbies, and attractiveness. Venus gives us the ability to notice the beauty in particular things and regulates our attraction to them.

Mars

Mars is the planet of "passion." Anger, sex, confidence/self-image, and motivation are all under its influence.

Jupiter

Jupiter is more concerned with the good elements of life, such as benevolence, personal development and wealth.

Saturn

Saturn is associated with the most difficult parts of life, such as loyalty, strength, ethical difficulties, productivity, and general life challenges.

Uranus

Uranus is the planet of "individualism." It battles for your authentic self, soul, and voice. Neptune is the planet of "dreams." It represents calm, tranquillity, and transformation and is linked to the core of sentient spirituality. Finally, Pluto is the planet of transition, and he is in charge of your destiny and the development of your highest self.

All of these planets interact with your natal chart, which includes the month, day, and time of your birth. Each planet will link to an astrological sign, which will indicate how you function in these specific areas. If my Mars planet is in Scorpio, for example, my personality's passion will be fierce and severe. This is due to the fact that Scorpios are already known for their passion and boldness.
